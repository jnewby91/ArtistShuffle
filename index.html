Artist Shuffle

Search. Listen. Discover.

  1. Enter a Music Artist in the Search Box
  2. Click Discover Music button
  3. Press play on audio track and a 30s preview a SIMILAR artist will be played
  4. Click the Recognize button if you know the Artist of the Song and a new track with another similar artist will be displayed
  5. Click the Don’t Recognize button if you don’t know the artist of the song and the song just listened to will be displayed
  6. Click Start Over at any time to enter another search
